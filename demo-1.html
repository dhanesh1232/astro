Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our Vedic astrology services.

How accurate are Vedic astrology predictions?

Vedic astrology has been practiced for over 5,000 years and is known for its precision. Accuracy depends on the exactness of birth details provided. Our readings have consistently received positive feedback from clients worldwide for their depth and relevance.

Can I get a consultation from outside India?

Absolutely. We serve clients in over 20 countries including the USA, UK, Australia, Canada, UAE, and Singapore. Consultations are delivered digitally, so location is never a barrier.

Is my information kept private?

Yes, your personal and birth details are kept strictly confidential. We do not share your information with any third parties. Your privacy and trust are our highest priority.

What if I don't know my exact birth time?

While an exact birth time gives the most precise reading, we can still provide valuable insights with an approximate time. We use rectification techniques to narrow down the ascendant and provide meaningful guidance.
