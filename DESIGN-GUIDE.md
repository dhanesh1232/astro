## Website Design Recommendations

### Hero Section Concept
- **Headline:** "Do Good. Know Your Stars."
- **Subheadline:** "Begin your cosmic journey with an act of kindness. Donate to a cause, then receive your personalized Vedic birth chart reading."
- **CTA 1:** "Start with Good Karma" (lemon yellow button → goes to charity selection)
- **CTA 2:** "Book Reading" (green outline button)
- **Form:** Integrated consultation form with all 10 fields
- **Visual:** Cosmic/spiritual imagery with warm lemon-gold tones

### User Flow
1. User lands on site → sees hero with "Good Karma" message
2. Selects a charity category (Goshala, Orphanage, Blind School, etc.)
3. Makes minimum donation ($3/£3/€3/₹108)
4. Gets redirected to consultation form (10 fields)
5. Submits → receives confirmation
6. Horoscope delivered within 1 day

### Sections to Include
1. **Hero** — Good Karma message + form preview
2. **How It Works** — Donate → Fill Form → Get Reading
3. **Charity Partners** — Grid of charity categories with descriptions
4. **Services** — What readings include
5. **Pricing** — Country-based pricing table
6. **Testimonials** — Global clients
7. **Trust Signals** — Years experience, countries served, readings delivered
8. **Footer** — Contact, WhatsApp, social links

### Dark/Light Theme
- **Light:** `#FFFDE7` cream background, `#1B5E20` green text, `#FDD835` yellow buttons
- **Dark:** `#111111` background, `#FDD835` yellow headings, `#4CAF50` lighter green for readability
